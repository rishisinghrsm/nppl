Welcome To N Pipeline Ltd. Pvt.



N Pipeline Ltd. Pvt., headquartered at Patna, Bihar is a wholly owned director company established in 2014. Expertise in Design & Engineering, all types of Pipeline, Station Piping & Civil Construction in a wide range of projects within various industries such as Oil & Gas Pipeline, crude, LPG, CNG, Chemicals within India.




Service Offer By Us


  • EPC / LSTK Projects
  • Cross Country / City Gas Distribution Pipeline
  • MG is an expert in cross-country pipeline construction for oil and gas infrastructure.
  • MG has the equipment’s, manpower, resources and expertise to schedule, manage, and construct pipelines of any size and length in all terrains. The Pipeline Construction activity includes the Residual Engineering, Supply of Pipes, Coating, Route Survey, Site Clearing & Grading, Stringing, Welding, NDT Testing, Joint Coating, Excavation, Lowering / Laying, Hydrostatic Testing, Pre-Commissioning and Commissioning of Pipeline System along with associated works such as execution of critical crossings by Augur boring and Horizontal Directional Drilling methods.
  • Station Construction (Fabrication & Errection) with As-built.
  • Horizontal Directional Drilling / Trenchless pipeline.
  • Manpower’s supply.
